Freefire

Free fire is a battle royale game which is available on android and ios. In this game you can customise your avatar. You should be the last man standing to win just like in other battle royales. There is a zone shrinking, if you are in the zone then your health will reduce slowly. There are 5 vehicles. There are multiple options in the game. It has three maps. You can buy pets, emotes to show off if you win and much more. You can play this with friends too. YOu just have to know their id.
